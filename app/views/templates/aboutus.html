About

Happystry is a platform for broadcasting happy deeds and responsible products.

We aspire to create a community (individuals, small entrepreneurs, corporates, social enterprises) that works towards a happier world. Happystry, we believe, is a step in this direction. Users can post content about the wonderful happy deeds they have been part of. Entrepreneurs can also showcase, sell, update their product listings across categories.

Some of the ways you can contribute towards building this community include

  • 1Signing up as a user to broadcast engaging content on various issues that you are passionate about.
  • 2Enlisting as a vendor to showcase and sell your socially responsible products.
  • 3Registering your company and broadcasting your CSR activities.
  • 4Enlisting your social enterprise to showcase great work on a social issue.
Through these interactions, we strive to create a happier connected world. Entrepreneurs can showcase their products to individuals and corporates.
Similarly, we enable corporates and social enterprises to reach out to a larger community.
These interactions and activities on the platform generate a currency "Dlites",
which going forward can be used for transactions both within the platform, and outside through our partner ecosystem.
